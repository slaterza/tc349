Nasa Moon Website Card Sort

My sort of Nasa's Moon website may have been a little scewed because I had looked it over but there were still a few things I grouped together differently. For the first group I have all the extra content that seemed somewhat irrelevant. The second group consists of learning material and the third group could also be learning material but to a lesser extent. My 4th group is full of the images, video and other media. The 5th group is all about the missions and the final group is the news, events and ways to connect people together about the moon.

My sort ended up having the fewest separate groups while my brother Josh's sort had the most. For the most part we all grouped the missions together and the images with videos. The cards I consider education are somewhat grouped together throughout everyone's sorts but are varied here and there. Altogether we had many similarities but each sort did have variations that made sense to me and only a few that seemed strange.
